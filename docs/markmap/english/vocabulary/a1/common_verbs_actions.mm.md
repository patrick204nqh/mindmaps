# Vocabulary

- A1
  - Common Verbs & Actions
    - Basic Actions
      - be
        - definition: to exist; to have a particular identity or role.
        - pronunciation: /biː/
        - part of speech: verb
        - examples:
          - I am a student.
          - They are happy.
        - synonyms: exist, live
        - antonyms: cease, die
        - related words: is, are, was, were, being, been
      - have
        - definition: to possess, own, or hold something.
        - pronunciation: /hæv/
        - part of speech: verb
        - examples:
          - She has a new car.
          - I have two brothers.
        - synonyms: own, possess
        - antonyms: lack, need
        - related words: has, had, having
      - do
        - definition: to perform an action or task.
        - pronunciation: /duː/
        - part of speech: verb
        - examples:
          - I do my homework every day.
          - What are you doing?
        - synonyms: perform, execute
        - antonyms: neglect, ignore
        - related words: does, did, done, doing
